Commitment to Diversity





Diversity at GT-Technologies



GT-Technologies is deeply committed to representing and reflecting the diversity of our Team, Partners, and communities with respect to their unique experiences, perspectives and viewpoints. To us, Diversity is inclusion, acceptance, honoring differences, and respect. As a certified LGBTE diverse company, we are intentional in our efforts to include as many candidates from diverse communities as possible.


We believe that our entire Team shares the responsibility of upholding our values of equity and inclusion. Our Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) team takes the lead in working to increase awareness and accountability and promote active allyship throughout the company.

Our vision for inclusion is to create a workplace that is inclusive and encourages everyone to show up as their authentic self. We focus on fostering a culture that values unique contributions to build greater understanding of our people, communities, and customers that empower us to succeed.
