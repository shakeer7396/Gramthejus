What is DevOps?

DevOps is a set of practices and culture that brings development and operations teams together to work more collaboratively towards a common goal. DevOps enables organizations to quickly and efficiently deliver high-quality software and services to their customers by leveraging automation, continuous integration, and continuous delivery.
